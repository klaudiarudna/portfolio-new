About me

Designing Clarity in Complex Digital Products

Let’s talk about how design can move your business forward.

I’m Klaudia - a Senior Product & Service Designer with over eight years of experience leading digital products from discovery to delivery.

I help teams simplify complex systems, align around user and business goals, and turn insight into tested, scalable solutions. My work bridges UX research, strategy, and hands-on product design, ensuring every idea can be built, measured, and improved.

I’ve partnered with brands like eBay Motors Group, UNiDAYS, and YouCanBook.me, helping teams deliver measurable impact across B2B and B2C web and mobile platforms. From defining MVPs and mapping customer journeys to prototyping, testing, and iterating high-fidelity designs, I focus on turning complexity into clarity - enabling teams to move faster, make confident decisions, and deliver real outcomes.

Before specialising in UX, I spent nearly ten years as a freelance designer, creating branding, marketing materials, and websites. This foundation gives me a holistic perspective - balancing user needs, business goals, and operational realities. My background in finance (MSc) also shapes the way I approach design: analytically, strategically, and with measurable results in mind.

I thrive in fast-paced, collaborative environments, helping teams align around shared goals, uncover automation opportunities, and design products that are not just functional, but meaningful. When I’m not designing, you’ll find me gardening, spending time with family, or exploring how AI can extend the craft of design.
