How to Create a Service Blueprint That Actually Gets Used

In this video: A step-by-step walkthrough of creating service blueprints that teams actually reference and update, not just create once and forget.

What You'll Learn

In this tutorial, I walk through the complete process of creating a service blueprint that becomes a living document your team actually uses. Drawing from real projects with UK SMEs, I share practical techniques that ensure your blueprint drives decisions rather than gathering digital dust.

Key Takeaways

  • How to scope your blueprint for maximum impact without overwhelming detail
  • Facilitation techniques that get stakeholders engaged and contributing
  • The right level of fidelity for different stages of the process
  • Setting up templates and rules that make updates easy
  • Measuring adoption and ensuring long-term use

Tools Mentioned

Throughout this video, I demonstrate using Miro for collaborative blueprinting, but the principles apply to any mapping tool you prefer. I also share some free template resources you can download and adapt for your own projects.
