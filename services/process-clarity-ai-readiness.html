Process Clarity & AI Readiness Assessment

“RPA software implemented without a careful assessment of your processes is like hiring a bigger team of new employees without knowing why the last team failed. You’ll be making the same errors, only faster.” Source: MetaSource, “Why RPA Projects Fail”

What it is

A 3–6 week assessment that makes your operations clear enough to support real decisions. You'll see where work gets stuck, what's driving cost and risk, and whether automation or AI genuinely earns its place.

This is for you if

  • Teams are buried in manual work, but no one can show where the time really goes
  • Different functions blame different problems, and leadership can't see the full system
  • You need numbers to unlock the budget, not just "this feels inefficient"
  • Legacy systems and workarounds are blocking scale or automation

If the problem sits entirely within one team or you already have a clean, agreed process map, this probably isn't the right service.

What you walk away with

  • End-to-end process map - executive view and operational detail
  • Cost, risk, and effort breakdown - where money, time, and rework actually concentrate
  • Prioritised decision roadmap - ranked by impact, feasibility, and organisational difficulty
  • Clear recommendation on AI or automation - and when simplification alone is the smarter move

Why this pays for itself

Most automation and AI projects fail because the process underneath was never made clear. This work reduces build size, risk, and long-term cost, and often uncovers savings before any technology is introduced.
